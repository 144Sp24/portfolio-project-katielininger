Orange Blossom (Grid, Abstract, White Space)

Three posters I created in my Typography course. The task was to pick two to three colors and a word or words that correlate and incorporate them into three different designs: one based on a grid, one that is abstract, and one showcasing a use of white space. I chose complimentary colors orange and green using the words "orange blossom."
